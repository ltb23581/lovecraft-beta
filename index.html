Process Overview

Project Details

Checkpoint One – Foundation and Research

  • Conducted client meetings with Love.Craft Athens to identify goals and needs.
  • Performed exploratory and secondary research on nonprofit artist platforms.
  • Created role contracts, project timeline, and initial PR/FAQ.
  • Gathered inspiration for accessible portfolio layouts.

Checkpoint Two – Concept and Wireframes

  • Designed initial wireframes and user flow for the artist portfolio hub.
  • Conducted user interviews and collected feedback from Love.Craft staff.
  • Built alpha version on Squarespace for early structure testing.
  • Defined brand colors, typography, and accessibility standards.

Checkpoint Three – Beta and Design Expansion

  • Developed beta prototype with refined visuals and navigation.
  • Tested portfolio usability and accessibility on mobile and desktop.
  • Added content placeholders for artist stories, artwork, and bios.
  • Analyzed website traffic and engagement metrics for design insights.

Checkpoint Four – Finalization and Handoff

  • Completed full Squarespace build with artist pages and cohesive design system.
  • Created update/handoff guide for Love.Craft staff.
  • Developed project trailer and final presentation materials.
  • Prepared for SLAM showcase and client site launch.
